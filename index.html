Streams After 10th

Explore the various streams students can choose after completing class 10th.

  • Science: The most favorable stream offering lucrative career options in engineering, medical, IT, and research roles. Develops critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Commerce: Ideal for students interested in finance, economics, and business-oriented careers like MBA, Chartered Accountant, and Investment Banking.
  • Arts / Humanities: Emerging as a popular choice for its diverse career options in mass media, journalism, humanities, and social sciences.

Career Options After 10th

Science Stream

Choose from various career paths in Science after 10th, including:

  • Engineering: Develop critical thinking and analytical skills for careers in B.E., B.Tech, and more.
  • Medical: Pursue MBBS, BDS, or other medical careers through competitive exams like NEET.
  • Polytechnic, ITI, and Paramedical: Options like Diploma courses, ITI certificates, and paramedical fields.

Commerce Stream

Explore high-paying careers like Chartered Accountant, MBA, and Financial Advisor.

  • Subjects include Business Economics, Accountancy, and Business Law.

Arts / Humanities Stream

Diverse career options in mass media, journalism, humanities, and social sciences.

  • Subjects include Sociology, Psychology, Literature, and Political Science.

Additional Career Options

  • Polytechnic: Courses like Mechanical, Civil, and Computer Engineering via diploma courses.
  • ITI (Industrial Training Institutes): Technical courses in areas like Computer Hardware, Civil, Mechanical, and Electrical.
  • Paramedical: Fields like Medical Laboratory Technology, Nursing Assistance, and Dialysis Technician.
  • Vocational Courses: Short-term job-centric courses in Software Development, Fashion Designing, and Medical Imaging.
